How it runs

Five stages. Three to six weeks.

1
Select

Your Rating names the three weakest dimensions. We pick the workflows behind them.

2
Discover

Interviews and observation. How the work actually moves — not the tidy version.

3
Redesign

The To-Be operating model: roles, decision rights, handoffs, and the founder taken out of the loop.

4
Align

The team walks the new system, argues with it, and owns it. Adoption is designed, not hoped for.

5
Embed

A 90-day implementation plan with owners — then a re-rating to prove the climb was real.
