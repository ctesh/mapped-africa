Questions

Before you start.

How is this different from the free Mapped Score?

The free Score is a quick self-assessment. The Operational Rating is evidence-based — you submit real documents and data, and the rating reflects what you can prove, not just what you say.

How long does it take?

About 45–60 minutes to complete the intake and gather evidence. Your rating is returned shortly after review.

Is my information safe?

Yes. Everything is held with consent and kept confidential. Data only ever contributes to benchmarking in anonymised, aggregate form, and never identifiably without your written permission.

What if my rating is low?

That's the point of starting — the roadmap shows exactly what to fix to climb, and the re-rating proves you did.

A higher rating does not guarantee better business performance — it indicates stronger operational visibility and readiness, based on the evidence reviewed. Methodology (dimensions, bands and logic) is published openly; specific weights are held. Benchmark figures become live once enough verified assessments exist.
