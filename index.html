The problem

Every organisation has two versions

Visible
  • Organisation chart
  • Policies
  • Systems
  • Strategy
  • KPIs
  • Reports
Invisible
  • Knowledge
  • Decision paths
  • Relationships
  • Dependencies
  • Workarounds
  • Institutional memory
  • Trust

The visible organisation explains what exists. The invisible organisation explains why it performs.
